About The Role

We are seeking a dedicated and knowledgeable Agronomist to provide expert technical guidance and support to our farming community and internal product teams. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in plant pathology or related fields, enabling them to diagnose crop health issues, develop effective management strategies, and contribute to our digital tools that predict and mitigate crop disease risks.

This role is critical in translating on-ground agricultural challenges into scalable tech solutions that empower farmers and enhance productivity across the agricultural value chain.

Key Responsibilities

You will bridge the gap between field-level agricultural science and our technology platforms, with a special focus on plant health:

  • Provide direct agronomic advisory to farmers on crop cultivation, soil health, nutrient management, irrigation, and IPM/IDM
  • Diagnose field-level crop disorders, with specialized focus on identifying bacterial, fungal, viral, and nematode diseases
  • Develop and curate localized content (advisories, blogs, treatment protocols) for farmer-facing mobile applications
  • Collaborate with software teams to improve disease diagnostic algorithms and forecasting models
  • Conduct field trials and demonstrations to validate recommended practices and new Agri-inputs
  • Collect, analyze, and report field data to track disease trends and outbreak patterns
  • Train internal teams and key farmer partners on best agronomic practices
  • Contribute to the development of decision support systems for crop protection
