About The Role

Kisaan InfoTech Limited is expanding its treasury operations with a systematic forex trading program focused on GBP/USD through GIFT City IFSC. We are seeking a disciplined, analytical, and experienced forex trader to serve as our Authorized Trading Officer (Primary).

This is NOT a speculative trading position. You will execute rule-based, algorithm-supported strategies under CFO supervision with strict risk management protocols. This role combines technical trading expertise with corporate treasury discipline.

Strategic Objective: Generate 300 pips per week average on GBP/USD to supplement company operating costs through Board-approved, risk-controlled treasury operations.

You will report directly to the CFO and have full operational authority to execute trades within approved parameters. This is a high-impact role where your expertise directly contributes to the company's financial stability and growth.

Key Responsibilities

  • Trade Execution: Execute forex trades on GBP/USD following the EMA26 Position Matrix strategy during London and London-NYC overlap sessions (12:30 PM - 9:30 PM IST)
  • Market Analysis: Conduct daily multi-timeframe technical analysis using EMA, MACD, KST, RSI, Ichimoku, and volatility indicators
  • Risk Management: Maintain strict position sizing (≤2% risk per trade), leverage limits (max 1:10), and drawdown controls (5% weekly limit)
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Active monitoring of open positions during London trading session with immediate escalation of issues
  • Performance Reporting: Maintain detailed trade logs, daily P&L updates, and compile comprehensive weekly performance reports
  • SOP Adherence: Follow the approved Standard Operating Procedure (EMA26 Position Matrix) with 100% compliance on entry/exit criteria
  • Algorithm Integration: Work with Pine Script alerts (TradingView) and MT5 Expert Advisor for automated risk management
  • News Monitoring: Track economic calendar (UK CPI, BoE decisions, US NFP, FOMC) and pause trading around high-impact events
  • Treasury Coordination: Collaborate with CFO on capital allocation, profit distribution, and monthly board reporting
  • Continuous Improvement: Analyze trade patterns, optimize strategy parameters, and contribute to SOP refinements
