POSITION OVERVIEW

The Digital Marketing & Content Specialist will play a critical role in building and amplifying Kisaan Infotech's digital presence across multiple platforms. This multifaceted position requires a creative and strategic individual who can develop compelling content, execute targeted advertising campaigns, and drive engagement with our diverse audience of farmers, agricultural stakeholders, and agribusiness professionals.

The ideal candidate will understand both digital marketing best practices and the unique nuances of agricultural sector communication.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
A. Social Media Management (30% of time)
Platform Management & Growth:
  • Manage company presence across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, and emerging platforms
  • Develop and maintain content calendars aligned with agricultural seasons and company objectives
  • Monitor, respond to, and engage with audience within 24 hours across all platforms
  • Track and analyze social media metrics, engagement rates, and audience demographics
  • Maintain brand voice consistency across all channels
Community Engagement:
  • Build and nurture online communities of farmers and agricultural professionals
  • Organize online events, webinars, and live sessions with agricultural experts
  • Address customer queries and concerns promptly and professionally
  • Collect customer feedback and testimonials for marketing use
B. Content Creation & Development (35% of time)
Visual Content Creation:
  • Design graphics, infographics, and visual assets using Canva, Adobe Photoshop, or similar tools
  • Create short-form videos (Reels, Shorts, Stories) showcasing farmer success stories and product features
  • Develop long-form video content for YouTube including tutorials and interviews
  • Capture and edit high-quality photographs from field visits and events
  • Create branded templates for consistent visual identity
Written Content Development:
  • Write compelling social media captions in English, Hindi, and Marathi
  • Create blog posts and articles on agricultural trends and farmer stories
  • Develop persuasive ad copy for social media campaigns
  • Write scripts for video content and promotional materials
  • Craft email marketing content and newsletters
C. Social Media Advertising & Campaign Management (25% of time)
Paid Advertising Execution:
  • Plan, create, and manage campaigns across Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, LinkedIn Ads, and Google Ads
  • Design ad creatives including images, videos, carousels, and collection ads
  • Configure campaign objectives for awareness, engagement, traffic, and conversions
  • Manage advertising budgets efficiently to maximize ROI
  • Conduct A/B testing of ad creatives, copy, and targeting strategies
Campaign Analytics & Optimization:
  • Monitor performance using Facebook Ads Manager, Google Analytics, and other tools
  • Track key metrics: CPM, CPC, CTR, conversion rates, and ROAS
  • Generate weekly and monthly performance reports with actionable insights
  • Optimize campaigns based on performance data
D. Marketing Strategy & Documentation (10% of time)
  • Collaborate with management to develop comprehensive digital marketing strategies
  • Coordinate with sales team for product launches and promotions
  • Maintain detailed records of content and campaigns
  • Prepare monthly reports summarizing growth, engagement, and ROI
  • Stay updated on digital marketing trends and best practices
