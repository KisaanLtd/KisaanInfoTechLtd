Investment Analyst

Corporate Investments | Agrotech and Fintech Focus

About The Role

Expanding our investment operations with a systematic analysis program focused on agrotech, fintech, and related sectors through strategic corporate initiatives. Seeking a disciplined, analytical, experienced investment analyst to serve as Primary Investment Analyst.

This is NOT a speculative investment role. Execute data-driven, research-supported evaluations under CFO supervision with strict due diligence protocols.

  • Strategic Objective: Identify and evaluate investment opportunities that generate sustainable returns to support company growth and innovation in agriculture technology.
  • Reporting: Directly to CFO with full operational authority within approved parameters. High-impact role.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct in-depth analysis of potential investments in agrotech, fintech, and emerging markets.
  • Perform reviews of economic indicators, industry reports, and competitor landscapes.
  • Evaluate investment risks (market volatility, regulatory changes, sector-specific challenges).
  • Track performance of existing investments and recommend adjustments.
  • Maintain investment logs, prepare P&L analyses, and generate stakeholder reports.
  • Ensure 100% compliance with approved Standard Operating Procedures.
  • Collaborate with finance, operations, and strategy teams.

Essential Skills & Competencies

  • Advanced financial modeling, valuation (DCF, comparables), forecasting, SWOT, scenario planning.
  • Proficiency in Bloomberg, advanced Excel (VBA), Python/R, Tableau/Power BI.
  • Strong critical thinking, risk management, attention to detail, ethical judgment.

Education & Qualifications

  • Preferred: CFA Level I+, FRM, CAIA, MBA Finance/Investment.
  • Minimum: Bachelor's in Finance, Economics or related + proven experience.
  • Required: Verifiable track record (min 12 months) of analytical reports, recommendations, or performance metrics.

Experience Requirements

  • Minimum 2 years in investment analysis or financial research.
  • Preferred: 2–5 years in equity research, venture capital, private equity, or corporate finance (agrotech/fintech exposure advantageous).
