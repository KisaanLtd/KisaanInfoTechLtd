⚙️ PINE SCRIPT IMPLEMENTATION

Example: Bullish Optimal Entry Logic
// Calculate entry score
bullishScore = 0.0
bullishScore := (keltnerBullish and stUptrend) ? bullishScore + 3 : bullishScore
bullishScore := kcInside_st ? bullishScore + 2 : bullishScore
bullishScore := ema9AboveSma26 ? bullishScore + 2 : bullishScore
bullishScore := priceAboveVwap ? bullishScore + 1.5 : bullishScore
bullishScore := risingVolume ? bullishScore + 1.5 : bullishScore
bullishScore := isWithinPrevRange ? bullishScore + 1 : bullishScore
bullishScore := (dailyPositionZone == 3 or dailyPositionZone == 4) ? bullishScore + 1 : bullishScore
bullishScore := lowVolatility ? bullishScore + 1 : bullishScore
bullishScore := bullish_crossover ? bullishScore + 1.5 : bullishScore
bullishScore := st_min_Inside_pdZone ? bullishScore + 1 : bullishScore
bullishScore := hasBullishMomentum ? bullishScore + 0.5 : bullishScore

// Determine entry quality
entryQuality = bullishScore >= 14 ? "OPTIMAL" :
bullishScore >= 10 ? "GOOD" :
bullishScore >= 7 ? "MODERATE" : "WEAK"

// Calculate optimal entry price
optimalEntry = bullishScore >= 14 ? st_min + (atr * 0.1) :
bullishScore >= 12 ? kclower + (atr * 0.2) :
bullishScore >= 10 ? prevSessionVwap : na

// Calculate stop loss
stopLoss = bullishScore >= 14 ? st_min - (atr * 0.5) :
bullishScore >= 12 ? kclower - (atr * 0.5) :
bullishScore >= 10 ? dailyLow : na

// Signal generation
enterShort = bearishScore >= 10 and close >= optimalEntry and close < stopLoss

// Plot
plotshape(enterShort, "Short Entry", shape.triangledown, location.abovebar,
color=entryQuality == "OPTIMAL" ? color.red :
entryQuality == "GOOD" ? color.orange : color.yellow,
size=size.small)

🌐 MARKET CONDITION ADAPTATIONS

Trending Markets

Strong Uptrend
Adjust Logic:
  • Reduce weight of isWithinPrevRange
  • Increase weight of hasBullishMomentum
  • Accept entries at price (SMA63) more readily
  • Allow dailyPositionZone = 1 or 2
Min Score: Lower to 9 for aggressive entries
Strong Downtrend
Adjust Logic:
  • Reduce weight of isWithinPrevRange
  • Increase weight of hasBearishMomentum
  • Accept entries at price (SMA63) more readily
  • Allow dailyPositionZone = 4 or 5
Min Score: Lower to 9 for aggressive entries

Range-Bound Markets

Consolidation Phase
Adjust Logic:
  • Increase weight of lowVolatility
  • Require kcInside_st = true
  • Entry only at extreme bands (st_min/st_max)
  • Reduce targets to price (SMA63)
Min Score: Increase to 13 (be selective)
Choppy Markets
Adjust Logic:
  • Require risingVolume = true
  • Wait for clear bullish_crossover or bearish_crossunder
  • Use time-based stops aggressively
  • Reduce position size by 50%
Min Score: Increase to 14 (optimal only)
