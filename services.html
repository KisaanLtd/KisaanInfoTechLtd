Service 01

Precision Farming Readiness Audit & Planning

Not sure where to start? We begin with a comprehensive assessment of your farm. Our experts visit your field, analyze your current practices, soil health cards, and goals to create a customized, phased Precision Farming roadmap tailored to your specific needs and budget.

📋 Key Deliverables

👨‍🌾

Ideal For:

Service 02

Drone-Based Crop Health & Monitoring Services

Our certified pilots use advanced multispectral drones to see what the naked eye can't. We capture high-resolution imagery to give you a complete picture of your field's health, enabling early intervention and higher yields.

🛸 Services Include:

  • NDVI Mapping: Identify pest infestations, water stress, and nutrient deficiencies early, before they become visible problems affecting your yield.
  • Aerial Spraying Service: Precise application of pesticides and liquid fertilizers, reducing chemical use by up to 30% and saving water while ensuring uniform coverage.
  • Stand Count & Emergence Analysis: Assess planting efficiency and predict potential yield from the very start of the season, allowing for corrective measures.
🌾

Ideal For:

All farmers looking to reduce scouting time, take proactive measures, and gain actionable insights about crop health without manual field walking.

🔬
Service 03

Soil & Field Scanning Services

Go beyond the static Soil Health Card. We use portable sensors and GPS-guided soil sampling to create dynamic, high-resolution maps of your field's variability, revealing hidden opportunities for optimization.

📊 Services Include:

  • Grid Soil Sampling: Create detailed maps for pH, Organic Carbon, and key nutrients (N, P, K) across multiple zones in your field for precision fertilizer application.
  • Real-Time Soil Sensor Installation: We install and monitor wireless sensors that provide live data on soil moisture and temperature, sent directly to your phone for instant decision-making.
🌱

Ideal For:

Farmers struggling with inconsistent soil quality and those wanting to optimize fertilizer application, reduce input costs, and improve soil health over time.

📈
Service 04

Data-Driven Farm Management Advisory

This is where data becomes decision. We are your personal farm advisors, using information from drones, sensors, and weather stations to give you hyper-local, timely advice that maximizes profitability.

💡 Advisory Includes:

  • Prescription Maps for VRT: Create detailed maps for your fertilizer spreader or sprayer to apply inputs only where needed, eliminating waste and reducing costs by 20-40%.
  • Precision Irrigation Scheduling: Get exact recommendations on when and how much to irrigate each block of your field based on real-time soil moisture and weather forecasts.
  • Pest & Disease Alert System: Receive early warnings and targeted treatment plans based on weather patterns, crop stage, and historical data from your region.
🎓

Ideal For:

Progressive farmers who want to move from reactive to proactive management, making data-backed decisions that increase yields and reduce risk.

Service 05

Custom Hiring Center & Implementation Support

We make advanced technology accessible. Through our network of partners, we facilitate access to rental precision farming equipment, ensuring that even small farmers can benefit from cutting-edge technology without heavy capital investment.

🤝 What We Offer:

  • GPS-Guided Tractor Attachments: Access to precision planters, variable rate spreaders, and auto-steer systems on a rental basis for critical farming operations.
  • Sensor-Based Irrigation Systems: Rent advanced drip and sprinkler systems with soil moisture sensors for the season without purchasing expensive equipment.
  • On-Ground Implementation Support: Our trained technicians provide hands-on support for implementing VRT and other precision techniques, ensuring you get maximum value from the technology.
👥

Ideal For:

Small and marginal farmers, and FPOs who cannot afford large capital investments but want to benefit from precision farming technology on a pay-per-use basis.
