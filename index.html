ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE?

  •  We are The Agritech, IT and ITeS Public Startup Company.

  •  The Peer To Peer Farmer & Agriculture Stakeholder Shared Service Platform.

  •  The Recruitment & Manpower Sourcing Agency.

  •  The Web Development & Digital Marketing Agency.

  •  The Business Coaching & Skill Development Agency.

  •  The Export House.

WHAT WE DO?

  •  We offer Farmers & Agriculture Stakeholders an omni channel shared Service platform to Rent / Buy / Sell / Hire / Invest For Agri Input items, Agri Services, AgriBusiness Projects and Agricultural Produce.

  •  We Promote and Facilitate Export, Co-Investing, Co-operative Farming and Agri Allied Businesses.

  •  We Design, Develop & Maintain Websites, Web & Mobile Applications

  •  We Source and Supply Manpower.

  •  We offer Business Coaching & Skill Development.
