## 4. JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

The Employee shall perform the following duties and responsibilities:

### 4.1 Backward Linkage Operations
- Coordinate with farmers for aggregation of agricultural produce
- Build and maintain relationships with farmer networks
- Facilitate procurement of farm produce as per quality standards and demand requirements
- Ensure timely collection of produce from farmers
- Verify quality parameters at procurement point

### 4.2 Logistics and Supply Chain Management
- Manage transportation of produce from farmer locations to storage facilities
- Coordinate movement of goods from storage facilities to customers
- Oversee loading and unloading operations
- Ensure proper handling to minimize damage and wastage
- Monitor cold chain requirements where applicable

### 4.3 Packaging and Branding
- Supervise packaging operations as per company standards
- Ensure proper labeling and branding of products
- Maintain packaging material inventory
- Coordinate with vendors for packaging supplies

### 4.4 Heavy Vehicle Driving
- Drive heavy vehicles for transportation of agricultural produce
- Ensure vehicle maintenance and compliance with transport regulations
- Maintain valid driving license for heavy vehicles (LMV/HMV)
- Follow all traffic rules and safety protocols

### 4.5 Documentation and Record Keeping
- Maintain accurate transaction records of procurement and sales
- Document quality standards compliance for each batch
- Keep records of farmer details, procurement quantities, and prices
- Prepare daily/weekly reports on logistics activities
- Maintain stock registers and movement records
- Update data in company systems/software as required

### 4.6 Coordination
- Liaise between backward linkage (farmer network) and forward linkage (marketing team)
- Communicate demand requirements to farmers
- Update marketing team on procurement status and availability
- Coordinate with storage facility managers

### 4.7 Other Duties
- Perform any other duties as assigned by the management from time to time
- Participate in training programs as required by the Company

### 13.1 Notice Period

- Gross misconduct or negligence
- Breach of confidentiality
- Theft, fraud, or dishonesty
- Violation of company policies
- Falsification of records or documents
- Conviction of a criminal offense
- Reckless driving causing damage or accident
- Consistent poor performance despite warnings
- Any act bringing disrepute to the Company

### 13.3 Resignation by Employee
The Employee may resign by submitting written notice as per the notice period requirement.

### 13.4 Relieving Process
Upon termination/resignation, the Employee shall:
- Serve the full notice period or pay salary in lieu
- Hand over all company property, documents, and records
- Complete all pending work and documentation
- Provide complete handover to designated person
- Obtain clearance from all departments
- Return ID card, keys, vehicle, mobile, and any other company assets
